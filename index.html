Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What are the check-in and check-out times?
Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability.

Is Hotel Mohan Inn a family hotel?
Yes, Hotel Mohan Inn is highly rated as a family hotel. We offer spacious family suites, kid-friendly menus in our restaurant, and a safe environment for all ages.

Do you offer a parking facility?
Absolutely. We provide a secure, complimentary parking facility for all guests staying with us, with 24/7 security.

Are all rooms AC rooms with free WiFi?
Yes, every room in our hotel is fully air-conditioned (AC rooms) and comes equipped with high-speed, unlimited free WiFi access.
